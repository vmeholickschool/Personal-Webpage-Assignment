St. Clair Shores is a city in Macomb County, Michigan. It is a suburb of Detroit.

My passions

When I am not working or attending bootcamp, I enjoy spending time around the house working on home improvements and caring for my 4 dogs and 2 cats and playing pinball on my Arcade 1up machine. Animal Rescue has been my greatest passion for the past 6 years. My favorite Musician is Chris Cornell, I love Tattoos and I love going to concerts.
